Who We Are

A Community
Rooted in Love

Couples for Christ (CFC) Singapore is a Catholic lay movement that has been serving families in Singapore for over 40 years. We are a community of couples, singles, youth, and children — each supporting the other on the journey of faith.

We believe that the renewal of the Church begins in the home — that when families are transformed by the love of Christ, society is transformed too.

Christ-centred living — Jesus at the heart of every home
Authentic community — families walking together in love
Servant hearts — caring for the poor and vulnerable
Meet Our Ministries
Our Model & Patron

The Holy Family
of Nazareth

Jesus, Mary, and Joseph are the heart of CFC. Their family in Nazareth — simple, faithful, and full of love — is the model every CFC family aspires to. We look to them not as distant figures, but as real people who show us how to love God and one another in the ordinary moments of daily life.

✝
Jesus — our Lord, Saviour, and brother; the reason we gather
✿
Mary — our mother and intercessor; model of faithfulness
◎
Joseph — protector, provider, and faithful servant of God's will
